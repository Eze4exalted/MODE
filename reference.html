Types of Website

For prospective bloggers or business intending to establish an online presence, knowing what type of website you want to launch is crucial. Hence, below are various types of website.

E-Commerce Website

An e-commerce website is any site designed to facilitate the sale and purchase of products and services. Web pages of an e-commerce site includes general pages found on most website (homepage, contact page and about page) and more importantly, the shopping cart and payment sections are essential features that constitute an e-commerce website.

Blog

A blog is a term used to describe websites that maintain an ongoing chronicle of information. Blogs are created for various reasons and focuses on one subject or a range of subjects.

Features of a blog
Blogs tend to have a few things in common (although there are exceptions):
  • A main content area with articles listed chronologically, the newest on top.
  • Links to articles on other websites.
  • A way for people to leave comments about the articles.
Some blogs may have additional features beyond these.
